The art of PITCH

Hi, my name is Rotem, and here is an example of a website that I built. I chose this topic of Pitch since I'm fascinated
on the psychological effect that a person can make on another just from looking how he/she presents, whereas the
content of the prentation may be almost irrelevant in some cases. It is something that everyone of us encounter
on a daily basis, and each one of us can improve and be good at..or in other words:

"Fake it till you make it"

Selling ideas to strangers can be challenging just as coming up with the ideas themselves.
Many times entrepreneurs, sales and marketing executives, going too far while explaining their product value
and get rejected by the decision makers who seem to miss the real value of the product.

Why does it happen?

The decision makers tend to give weight to the pitcher’s creativity and presentation skills,
higher than assumed, which might overshadow the idea’s worth.

So how should you present your idea?
